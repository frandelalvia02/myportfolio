01 — ABOUT

New to VA work. Not new to the work itself.

I'm Frandel, an order management and customer support specialist based in Imus, Philippines, now building a career as an independent Virtual Assistant.

For 11 years I worked on the backend of a major telecom account — processing orders, auditing transactions, tracking devices against stock, and keeping CRM records clean enough that nothing fell through the cracks. It's the same job a good VA does for a small business: order accuracy, inventory awareness, and a customer who always knows where their order stands.

I'm new to freelancing, but not to the responsibility. The sample projects below are practice work I built to show exactly how I'd approach a real client's order and support workflow.

Order tracker for a small online supply shop

Practice Project
Scenario

A fictional shop, Coastal Supply Co., needed a simple way to track orders from "placed" to "delivered" without expensive software.

What I built

A Google Sheets order tracker with status columns, conditional formatting for overdue orders, and a matching intake form.

Outcome

Reduced the manual back-and-forth of "where's my order" into a single glance-able view, updatable in under a minute per order.
